# Company Handbook

Welcome to TechCorp! This handbook outlines our policies and procedures.

## Security Training
All employees must complete security training annually. This training covers:
- Password best practices
- Phishing awareness
- Data handling procedures
- Incident reporting

## Communication
- Slack is the approved internal chat platform
- Email for external communication
- Microsoft Teams for video calls

## Work Hours
- Standard hours: 9 AM to 6 PM
- Flexible hours available with manager approval
- Core hours: 10 AM to 4 PM (mandatory presence)
